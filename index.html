Microsoft was once just an idea.


In 1975, Bill Gates and Paul Allen had a huge dream - a computer on every desk and in every home. At the heart of that vision was our mission of empowerment,to everyone and help people from all walks of life create, prosper, and achieve. Today, our mission driving us forward over the next forty years. And beyond.



We love people, with dreams too!


Microsoft is about people. We believe in what people make possible. We stand in awe of what people dare to achieve. We aim to help them in all aspects of their lives. Everything we do is grounded in this belief. It's why we come to work, why we innocate and why we create technology that empowers people to thrive, in ordinary and extraordinary ways, every single day.
